About me

Hi, everyone. I'm k-e034, a font creator who draws characters just for my joy. I'm a student at a university in Tokyo, Japan. This font you're now looking is "Good Day Sans" which is my first original font. I am providing several digital font files on the Internet as "E-Type Design".

What's E-Type Design?

E-Type Design is a font vendor (or provider) which I am running. "E" at the beginning of the name stands for several meanings. In spoken Japanese language, which is my mother tongue, a word "良い" (litterally means "good") is pronounced "ee" just like pronouncing "E" in English. Also, the initial letter for my family name is "E", so I put the letter at the beginning of this font vendor/provider name.
